How DAO Governance Works

Creating Proposals
  • Any member can create a proposal
  • Describe changes or ideas for the community
  • Set voting duration (default 7 days)
  • Proposals are visible to all members
Voting System
  • Voting power = your token balance
  • One vote per user per proposal
  • Vote FOR or AGAINST proposals
  • Proposals pass if FOR votes exceed AGAINST
Decentralized: All decisions are made by the community through transparent voting. Your tokens give you a voice!
